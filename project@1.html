PROJECT




AUTOMATIC POTHOLE DETECTION AND CLEARENCE ROBOT


ABSTRACT

The preservation of roads is one of the most important issues in emerging nations. The economy of the nation is significantly boosted by well-maintained roadways. Indian secondary roads frequently have potholes, some of which may be high depth at that time we will get the gps values through app for clearance. Therefore, it is crucial to spot potholes and gauge their depths in both situations to ensure safe driving. In this project, we create a sensor model based on the arduino, which allows for such detection and depth estimates utilizing. Robot is controlled through wifi app and monitoring by esp32 camara
THE MAIN OBJECTIVE OF MY PROJECT IS TO REDUCE COOLISION OF VECHILES AND CLEAR THE POTHOLE OF ROAD AUTOMATICALLY.
